General Questions

How long does it take to build a construction website?

+
Most websites are completed within 2–4 weeks after homepage approval. The exact timeline depends on the number of pages and project complexity. We always provide a clear timeline during your discovery call.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

+
Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates and improvements, security monitoring, daily/weekly backups, and content edits. This ensures your website stays secure, fast, and fully optimized.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+
Absolutely. Every website is built mobile-first and fully responsive. Since most construction leads come from mobile devices, we prioritize fast mobile loading speed, easy navigation, and click-to-call functionality.

Can you help with high-quality images for my projects?

+
Yes. We can recommend professional photographers in Houston, guide you on capturing high-converting project photos, and optimize existing or stock images for better presentation.

How do you handle local SEO for construction companies?

+
We implement a complete local SEO strategy including Houston-specific keyword targeting, Google Business Profile optimization, local citations and directory listings, and service-area landing pages. This helps your business appear when customers search for your services.

Technical Questions

What technologies do you use?

+
We use modern, high-performance web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and WordPress (custom-built themes when needed). Every site is built for performance, SEO, and scalability.

Will you take care of my website after launch?

+
Yes. We offer full post-launch support and ongoing maintenance plans to handle updates, fixes, improvements, and security monitoring.

How do you ensure my website loads quickly?

+
We optimize every site using image compression, code minification, caching strategies, and performance-focused hosting. All websites are tested to achieve fast load speeds (under 2 seconds where possible).

Is hosting included?

+
Hosting is optional and starts at $99/month, which includes managed hosting, security monitoring, and daily backups. You may use your own hosting, but we recommend ours for best performance and support.

Do you provide SSL security?

+
Yes. Every website includes a free SSL certificate to secure your site and improve Google rankings.

Trust & Confidence

Do you specialize in construction websites?

+
Yes. Over 80% of our clients are contractors, including roofing companies, remodelers, pool builders, and general contractors. We understand your industry deeply.

Will my website help generate more leads?

+
Yes — that’s the entire purpose of our builds. We focus on conversion-driven layouts, strategic call-to-actions, lead capture systems, and local SEO optimization. Our goal is simple: turn traffic into real jobs.

Do you understand the construction industry?

+
Yes. We understand how contractors get leads, seasonal demand (storm season, peak remodeling cycles), and customer decision-making behavior. This allows us to design websites that actually perform in your market.

Will my website look professional and premium?

+
Yes. Every design is fully custom (no templates), modern and high-end, and built to position you as a trusted contractor in Houston.

Do I own my website?

+
Yes. Once the project is fully paid, you own your website design, all content, and all assets. We also provide full access credentials.

Pricing & Process

What’s included in your pricing?

+
Each package includes custom website design, mobile optimization, SEO foundation, lead generation structure, and a defined page count. See our Pricing page for full details.

+
Yes. We offer flexible options: 50% upfront / 50% on completion, or split monthly payments (case-by-case).
